What is the EB-5 Investment Visa Programme

Your Gateway to US Permanent Residency

The EB-5 Immigrant Investor Programme provides foreign nationals and their families a direct pathway to US permanent residency (green card) through qualifying investments in American businesses. Established by Congress in 1990, this programme stimulates the US economy through job creation and capital investment from foreign sources.

Investment Requirement

Invest $800,000 USD in a Targeted Employment Area (TEA) or $1,050,000 in a standard area through an approved regional centre or direct business

Job Creation

The investment must create or preserve at least 10 full-time jobs for qualified US workers within two years of investor admission

Green Card Path

Investors and their immediate family (spouse and unmarried children under 21) receive conditional green cards, leading to permanent residency

No Sponsorship Required

Unlike employment-based visas, EB-5 requires no employer sponsorship, offering complete freedom in career and location choices

Family Benefits

Spouses can work anywhere in the US, and children gain access to world-class American education without international student restrictions or fees

Path to Citizenship

After maintaining residency for five years, EB-5 investors become eligible to apply for US citizenship

Permanent Solution

EB-5 provides permanent residency, not a temporary visa, offering long-term security and stability for your family's future

Complete EB-5 Process Guide

From Investment to Green Card Approval

Initial Consultation & Evaluation

Duration: 1-2 weeks

Comprehensive assessment of your financial qualifications, immigration goals, and family circumstances. We review source of funds documentation and provide preliminary project recommendations tailored to your objectives.

Project Selection & Due Diligence

Duration: 2-4 weeks

Detailed evaluation of available regional centre projects, including business plans, job creation models, developer track records, and risk assessments. We conduct thorough due diligence to ensure project viability and compliance.

Investment & Escrow

Duration: 2-3 weeks

Preparation of investment agreements and transfer of funds to USCIS-approved escrow accounts. Your capital remains protected until I-526 petition approval, ensuring security throughout the process.

I-526 Petition Filing

Duration: 3-6 months preparation

Compilation of comprehensive petition package including source of funds documentation, business plans, and economic reports. Our team works with immigration attorneys to ensure complete, accurate submissions.

I-526 Approval & Visa Processing

Duration: 24-36 months

USCIS reviews and approves your petition. Upon approval, you proceed through consular processing at the US Embassy in India or adjustment of status if already in the US.

Conditional Green Card

Duration: 2 years

You and your family receive conditional green cards valid for two years. During this period, you establish US residency whilst the regional centre project continues creating requisite jobs.

I-829 Petition & Permanent Residency

Duration: 18-24 months

Within 90 days before your conditional green card expires, we file Form I-829 to remove conditions. Upon approval, you receive permanent, unrestricted green cards for your entire family.

Total Timeline: The complete EB-5 process typically takes 4-6 years from initial investment to permanent green card. However, processing times vary based on USCIS workload and country-specific visa availability for Indian nationals.
