Your EB-5 Journey, Simplified

A transparent, step-by-step process from consultation to green card

Initial Consultation & Assessment

Project Selection & Due Diligence

Access our curated portfolio of USCIS-approved regional center projects. Our team conducts comprehensive due diligence and provides detailed risk analysis reports.

Source of Funds Documentation

Our experts guide you through gathering and organizing all required financial documentation. AI-powered validation ensures completeness before submission.

3

I-526E Petition Filing

Meticulous preparation and filing of your I-526E petition with USCIS. Track every stage through our real-time dashboard with estimated processing timelines.

4

Conditional Green Card

Upon I-526E approval, receive your conditional green card and begin your life in the United States. We provide relocation support and settlement services.
